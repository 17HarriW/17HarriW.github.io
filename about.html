After finishing my GCSEs, I secured an array of 9s (equivalent to A**) including Computer Science, Mathematics, and English Language.
Presently, during my A levels, my passion for computer science is greater than ever. I have expanded my repertoire to encompass a wide breadth of programming languages, including C#, JavaScript, Python, and SQL. Armed with this knowledge, I am now more adept at creating software than ever and remain eager to learn and develop further.
