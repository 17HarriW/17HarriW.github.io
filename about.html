My life, so far:

My fascination with the world of design and coding began at a tender age of 7 when I joined a coding club. It was here that I experienced an immediate connection with the computational approach to problem-solving, which seamlessly blended with the logical workings of my mind. I transitioned from creating fun and interactive Scratch games to venturing into the world of web development using HTML and CSS, all before completing primary school. The ability to craft websites, visually appealing and functional, opened up new possibilities in my creative journey.
As I entered secondary school, my thirst for knowledge led me to Python, which fueled my passion for computer design even further. I found joy in constructing intricate programs and exploring the depths of software architecture. The culmination of my efforts came to fruition when I achieved outstanding results in my GCSEs. With flying colors, I secured an array of 9s (equivalent to A**) including Computer Science, Mathematics, and English Language. These accomplishments reinforced my belief in the path I had chosen and served as a testament to my dedication to the subjects I am truly passionate about.
Presently, during my A levels, my passion for computer science burns brighter than ever. I have expanded my repertoire to encompass a wide breadth of programming languages, including C#, JavaScript, Python, and SQL. Armed with this knowledge, I am now adept at creating software of remarkable quality, with the potential to be offered as marketable products.
